<span style="color:blue">**Statistical Analysis of Spatial Patterns**</span> <br>
<i>How can we measure the relationship between two sets of spatial objects?</i> <i>Is this measure robust to stochastic association between the data points? </i>
At Institut Pasteur, we are developing statistical tools to address such problems in Super-Resolution Microscopy and Histopathology. Find more details of our work on [Generalized Statistical Object Distance Analysis](_gsoda.md) webpage
